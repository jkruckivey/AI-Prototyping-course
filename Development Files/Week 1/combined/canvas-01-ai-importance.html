AI: Concepts, Context, and Fit

What is AI (vs. traditional automation)?

What is AI (vs. traditional automation)?

AI refers to systems that learn patterns from data and improve with experience. Traditional automation follows explicit, hand-written rules.

Comparison of rule-based automation and AI/ML
Aspect Rule-based Automation AI / Machine Learning
How it works IF/THEN rules crafted by experts Models learn patterns from examples
When to use Stable logic, few exceptions Complex patterns, variability, ambiguity
Maintenance Change rules manually Retrain with new data; monitor drift

Where AI fits in work (automation, assistance, augmentation)

Where AI fits in work (automation, assistance, augmentation)
  • Automation — routine tasks (routing, classification) executed consistently at scale.
  • Assistance — copilots for drafting, summarizing, and retrieval.
  • Augmentation — decision support: forecasts, risk scores, next-best actions.

Business value & constraints

🤖 Rule-Based Systems
Following Explicit Instructions

How it worked:

Programmers wrote explicit rules for every possible scenario. The system could only respond to situations that were explicitly programmed.

Temperature Control: IF temperature > 30°C THEN turn_on_AC
Chess Move: IF piece = "knight" AND move = "L-shape" THEN valid_move
Simple Logic: IF raining = true THEN carry_umbrella

What it could do:

✅Play simple games (checkers, tic-tac-toe)
✅Solve mathematical problems
✅Follow precise instructions
❌Adapt to new situations
❌Learn from experience
🧠 Expert Systems
Capturing Human Expertise

How it worked:

Captured human expert knowledge in specific domains. Knowledge engineers interviewed experts to create comprehensive rule bases.

Medical Diagnosis: IF symptoms = "fever + cough + chest_pain" THEN likely_pneumonia
Financial Advice: IF age < 30 AND risk_tolerance = "high" THEN recommend_stocks
Fault Diagnosis: IF machine_temp = "high" AND oil_pressure = "low" THEN shutdown_required

What it could do:

✅Medical diagnosis assistance
✅Financial recommendations
✅Explain reasoning process
✅Handle complex domain knowledge
❌Handle completely new scenarios
❌Learn automatically from data
📊 Machine Learning
Learning from Data

How it works:

Systems learn patterns from data instead of following pre-programmed rules. Algorithms discover relationships and make predictions based on training examples.

Email Spam Detection: Analyzes thousands of emails to learn spam patterns automatically
Product Recommendations: Studies purchase history to suggest relevant items
Credit Scoring: Uses historical data to predict loan default risk

What it can do:

✅Recognize complex patterns
✅Adapt to new data
✅Handle uncertainty and noise
✅Make probabilistic predictions
❌Always explain every decision
❌Handle complex unstructured data
🧬 Deep Learning
Neural Networks at Scale

How it works:

Artificial neural networks with multiple layers process vast amounts of unstructured data. These deep networks can learn hierarchical representations and handle complex patterns.

Image Recognition: Identifies objects, faces, and scenes in photos with human-level accuracy
Natural Language: Understands context, generates human-like text, and translates languages
Content Generation: Creates realistic images, videos, and text from simple prompts

What it can do:

✅Process images, speech, and text
✅Generate new creative content
✅Understand context and nuance
✅Transfer learning across domains
❌Always provide explainable reasoning
❌Guaranteed consistent behavior
