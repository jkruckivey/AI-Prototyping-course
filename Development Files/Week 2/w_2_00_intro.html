 Welcome to Week 2

What this week is about

We dive deep into supervised learning: linear & logistic regression, KNN, SVM, Naive Bayes, Decision Trees & Random Forests, and a first look at Neural Networks. You’ll focus on when to use each, how to evaluate them, and the business trade‑offs behind thresholds and hyperparameters.

 Before you start

Readiness checklist

  • You can tell classification (categorical target) from regression (numeric target).
  • You know what parameters (learned) and hyperparameters (set before training) are.
  • You understand why train/validation splits (often time‑aware) matter.

Need a refresher? Jump back to Week 1: Model families · Workflow · Evaluation basics

What you'll do this week

  • Explore the intuition behind each algorithm.
  • Use interactive widgets to see how metrics and boundaries change.
  • Make business‑aligned choices (e.g., thresholds tied to cost, feature scaling for KNN/SVM).

 Warm‑up: Task → Model Type

Kick off with a quick matching activity to activate prior knowledge.

 How to get the most out of Week 2

  • Benchmark first: compare new models to simple baselines (mean predictor, logistic regression, shallow tree).
  • Mind the splits: for temporal data, use time‑aware validation to avoid leakage.
  • Pick metrics that match the decision: precision/recall for rare events; MAE/RMSE for numeric targets; align thresholds with cost.
