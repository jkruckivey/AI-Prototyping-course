 Week 4 — Orientation

What this week is about

In this module, we shift focus from choosing models to evaluating and training them effectively. You’ll learn how to measure fit, prevent overfitting, and connect evaluation metrics directly to business outcomes.

 Before you start

Readiness checklist

  • You can distinguish classification vs regression tasks.
  • You know the basics of train/validation/test splits.
  • You are familiar with core supervised algorithms (linear/logistic regression, trees, SVM, etc.).

Need a refresher? Jump back to: Evaluation Basics (Module 1) · Module 2 Wrap-Up

What you'll do this week

  • Compare evaluation metrics and know when to use each (accuracy, precision/recall, ROC, etc.).
  • Understand overfitting vs underfitting and how to mitigate them.
  • Practice cross-validation techniques and align metrics with business KPIs.

 Warm‑up: Why Evaluation Matters

Think about the following question: What could go wrong if we only looked at accuracy when judging a fraud detection model? Jot down your thoughts before moving on.

 How to get the most out of Week 4

  • Look beyond accuracy: Match metrics to business costs (false positives vs false negatives).
  • Think in curves: ROC and PR curves help visualize tradeoffs.
  • Validate properly: Use cross-validation or time-based splits to avoid leakage.
