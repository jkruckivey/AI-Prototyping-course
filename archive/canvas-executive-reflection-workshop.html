Executive Reflection Exercise

Workshop Objective

This hands-on activity helps you identify specific AI opportunities within your organization. You'll move from identifying pain points to prioritizing AI solutions based on impact and feasibility, creating a concrete roadmap for AI implementation.

Step 1: Pain Point Analysis

Duration: 8 minutes | Activity: Individual reflection and documentation
Identify Your Organization's Key Challenges

Identify three significant challenges your organization faces. These might be inefficiencies, customer experience gaps, operational bottlenecks, or competitive disadvantages.

Framework for Pain Point Identification:
  • Operational Inefficiencies: Repetitive tasks, manual processes, slow decision-making
  • Customer Experience Issues: Long response times, generic experiences, service gaps
  • Data and Insights: Limited visibility, poor forecasting, reactive decision-making
  • Competitive Disadvantages: Higher costs, slower innovation, market share loss

Step 2: Competitive Intelligence Research

Duration: 7 minutes | Activity: External research and benchmarking
Research Industry AI Solutions

Research how companies in your industry use AI to solve similar problems. Use case studies, reports, or press releases. Look for specifics, not just general claims.

Research Framework:

For each pain point you identified, find:

Competitive Intelligence Research Framework
Research Category What to Find Example Sources
Company Examples Organizations using AI for similar challenges Industry reports, case studies, press releases
Solution Types Specific AI approaches or technologies used Vendor case studies, technology blogs
Results Achieved Measurable outcomes, ROI, efficiency gains Published metrics, analyst reports
Research Tips:
  • Focus on companies of similar size and complexity
  • Look for specific metrics rather than vague claims
  • Note implementation timelines and resource requirements
  • Identify potential vendors or solution providers
