Strategic Insight:

Organizations that master all four dimensions create sustainable competitive advantages. Volume provides comprehensive insights, Velocity enables rapid response, Variety reveals hidden patterns, and Veracity ensures reliable decision-making. Companies focusing on only one or two dimensions miss opportunities for integrated business intelligence.
