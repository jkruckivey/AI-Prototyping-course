Executive's 6-Step Data Framework

Click "Next Step" to reveal each phase and the executive's key role

Step 1 Collect ▶
Executive Role: Define what matters

Focus on business-critical data sources that directly impact strategic decisions. Quality over quantity.

Timeline: 2-3 weeks
Step 2 Clean & Organize ▶
Executive Role: Set standards

Establish data quality standards that enable reliable AI insights and business decisions.

Timeline: 4-6 weeks
Step 3 Analyze ▶
Executive Role: Ask the right questions

Guide analysis toward business outcomes that create competitive advantage and measurable value.

Timeline: 2-4 weeks
Step 4 Model ▶
Executive Role: Approve priorities

Balance innovation with risk, ensuring AI models serve strategic business objectives.

Timeline: 6-8 weeks
Step 5 Act ▶
Executive Role: Champion adoption

Drive organizational change and ensure AI insights translate into improved business processes.

Timeline: 4-6 weeks
Step 6 Monitor ▶
Executive Role: Review performance

Continuously evaluate business impact and adjust strategy to maximize competitive advantage.

Timeline: Ongoing
